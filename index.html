Algae Biofuel Instructions

Adding Water

Add 5 gallons of distilled water to a 10 gallon water tank. Then attach a water heater to the tank, we used the Anova Precision Cooker. After that set tempature to 75째f(23.8째c).

Adding Algae

Once the water is at 75째f(23.8째c) add your algae we used was Algae Research and Supply Chorella Pyrenoidosa Check up on it and make sure its growing everyday, let it grow for 7-10 days.

Extracting Algae

Layer a funnel then filter paper then another funnel ontop of a flask. Once this set-up is constructed pour algae water into the funnel and let it filter.

Mixing in hexane

Once the algae is filter take a small sample of the algae around 20ml mix it with 100ml of hexane. Stir and wait a day for it to set. Make sure to have a secure lid where no air can get in.
